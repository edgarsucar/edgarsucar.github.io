Edgar Sucar

I am Postdocotral Fellow in the VGG at the Univiersity of Oxford working with Prof. Andrea Vedaldi, before that I was PhD student in the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial College London under the supervision of Prof. Andrew Davison.

I am generally interested in developing methods for 3D/4D scene understanding that enable machines to have meaningful interactions with their environment. I like building practical real-time systems which work in the real world.
